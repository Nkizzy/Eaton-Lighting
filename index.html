What we do

Thoughtful layouts, quality fixtures, and installs built to look great night after night.

  • Facade & architectural accent

    Uplighting, wash lighting, and focal accents that highlight texture, entryways, and rooflines without overpowering the neighborhood.

  • Landscape & path lighting

    Safe, welcoming walks, driveway edges, and garden features — balanced beam spread and color temperature for a cohesive look.

  • Design & consultation

    We walk the property with you, talk priorities and budget, and propose a plan before anything goes in the ground.

  • LED upgrades & maintenance

    Retrofits for efficiency and longevity, plus seasonal check-ins and adjustments so your system stays sharp.
